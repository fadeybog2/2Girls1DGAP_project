Welcome to the Bowling for your life!
We've put a lot of time, effort an love into this project.
Hope you'll like it!
It's a game about bowling and having fun. 
Controls:
A - move left
D - move right
W - jump
alt - fireball
To harm the pins, you can either jump on them or 
throw a fireball at them.
Watch out for thorns! They instantly kill bowling balls
Good luck and don't mess it up!
